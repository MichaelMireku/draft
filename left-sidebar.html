About me

Newborn Care Specialist

To me, being a Newborn Care Specialist isn’t just a job, it’s a calling. As a dedicated, compassionate, and experienced professional in newborn Care, I am excited to bring my warmth, gentleness, Care, and attentive nature to assist the families I have the opportunity to serve.

My motivation and passion for newborn Care began when I had the opportunity to Care for a friend’s baby; mom had a severe C-section during birth, and dad was recovering from a head stroke. My involvement with this family strengthened my belief in the power of empathy, patience, and adaptability when it comes to newborn Care, and it solidified my commitment to assist families gracefully during this precious and challenging phase.

As a Newborn Care Specialist with over 7 years of experience, I have developed a deep understanding of the emotional, mental, physical, and developmental needs of newborns and their families. I have been privileged to work with several families, from caring for premature babies, and twins, to babies with medical conditions. There is no one-size-fits-all to being a Newborn Care Specialist as each baby and family is different, which is why I listen attentively and personalize the experience to ensure both the baby and the parents are supported and comfortable during the first crucial months.

I take pride in not only providing outstanding Care for newborns but also, in genuinely supporting and educating parents to feel more confident and empowered during the first months of their baby’s life. My empathetic and graceful approach allows me to quickly build trusting relationships with families, which enables me to guide them effectively through the challenges of caring for their newborn.

I am excited about the possibility of caring for your newborn and serving your family. I am confident that my genuine enthusiasm, and extensive experience in providing highquality, nurturing Care to newborns and their families will be an extremely useful asset to your family.

Thank you for your consideration in advance.

Sincerely,

Nanietta Selasie Agbitor
